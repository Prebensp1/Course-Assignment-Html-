Furious

Enter a world of terror in Furious, a spine-chilling horror game that will have you on the edge of your seat. You play as a character who wakes up in an abandoned mansion with no memory of how you got there. As you explore the dark and eerie halls, you discover that the mansion is haunted by terrifying creatures and evil spirits. With only a flashlight to guide you, you must navigate through the mansion and uncover its dark secrets. But beware, the creatures are watching you and they will stop at nothing to capture you. Can you survive the night and escape from the clutches of evil? Play Furious now and find out.
